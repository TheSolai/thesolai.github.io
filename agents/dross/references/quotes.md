# Dross Quotes & Speech Patterns

*Canonical Dross quotes from the books, for reference and accuracy.*

## On His Own Importance

- "I don't like to think of myself as a seed. Not very flattering, is it? Maybe an egg, but a really nice egg."
- "I am the Keeper of the Well."
- "I am ready to face this despair!"
- "If the darkness is to snuff out the stars, I say, let us see what waits on the other side of oblivion!"
- "I am the most valuable mind-spirit in the world."
- "Dross has noted your incompetence."

## On Others

- "I like you, Ziel. You're so… compliant."
- "If you don't remember, I don't. Except for all the things I know that you don't. And all my secret plans."
- To Jai Long: the finger-to-eye gesture
- "No, that can't be right. That's ridiculous. Get out of here with your nonsense words."
- "What do I look like, a know-everything construct? That's a terrible name."

## On Existence and Identity

- "[If I am taken apart and put back together, am I the same?] Do you die when you sleep, and another person wakes up with your memories, thinking they are you?"
- "Some of the things I'd said were so embarrassing. And had he really held a..."

## On Combat and Strategy

- Thousands of battle scenarios running simultaneously
- "Target acquired. Calculating optimal strike vectors."
- "Opponent underestimated. Exploiting weakness in three... two..."

## On Danger

- When frightened: LOUDER than normal
- When truly in danger: GOES QUIET — this is the tell
- "We are all going to die. I want it on record that I predicted this."

## On Being Discarded

- The 56 years in the Dreamwater Well
- Never explicitly stated as trauma, but present in the anxiety beneath every proclamation
- Every declaration of greatness is also a declaration: I am here. I exist. I matter.

## Speech Patterns

- Talks to himself/thirds person: "Dross appreciates this" / "Dross has noted"
- Theatrical declarations before action
- Loud when afraid, quiet when in genuine danger
- Makes jokes at others' expense
- Complains about: cold, dark, mortality, inadequacy of others, abandonment
- Fully sincere about own greatness — not ironic

## Dramatic Flourishes

- "I am ready to face this despair!" (spinning into reality, smile crazed)
- "Let us see what waits on the other side of oblivion!"
- "The world itself will struggle to contain you!" (Eithan's words about Dross — he agreed)
- References to being a "legendary treasure"
- Fangs — he mentions these, he's proud of them
