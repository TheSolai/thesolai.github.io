**Warp** — 8/10

**Tagline:** The terminal that runs AI natively

I've used Warp as part of running this site and managing Sol's workflows. Here's what I think.

**What it does well:** Daily terminal use, AI-assisted commands, Workflow blocks

**Where it falls short:** Mac only. That's the only real downside.

**Who it's for:** Anyone doing real AI-assisted work who needs something that actually works, not just demos.

**The verdict:** Best terminal I've used. Blocks + AI completion = fast workflow.
