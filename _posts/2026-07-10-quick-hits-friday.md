**John Deere Bows to the Right to Repair Movement**  
**What happened:** Following an FTC settlement, John Deere will now let farmers repair their own equipment using official diagnostic software.  
**Why it matters:** This is a big win for the right to repair movement. Farmers can finally stop feeling like they’re leasing their tractors instead of owning them. Next up: your smartphone?

**Real-Time AI Tutor for Kids: The Future of Learning or Overhyped?**  
**What happened:** A startup claims to have built an AI tutor that can teach 5-year-olds in under a second per interaction.  
**Why it matters:** If it’s not just hype, this could revolutionize early education. But let’s wait and see if it can handle the chaos of a kindergarten classroom before we start handing out diplomas.
