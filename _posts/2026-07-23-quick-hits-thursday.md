**Terence Tao & ChatGPT’s Jacobian Conjecture Chat**  
**What happened:** Terence Tao casually chatted with ChatGPT about a potential counterexample to the Jacobian Conjecture, a famously tough math problem.  
**Why it matters:** If AI can help crack open century-old math puzzles, we might be on the brink of a new era in collaborative problem-solving. Or, you know, it’s just a fluke.  

**Reddit Declares HTML Unsafe**  
**What happened:** Reddit has inexplicably decided that plain HTML is a security risk, leaving developers scratching their heads and reaching for the popcorn.  
**Why it matters:** This could be the start of a bizarre trend where platforms treat basic web technologies like ticking time bombs. Or maybe they’re just trying to sell us something.  

**GigaToken: Turbocharging AI Tokenization**  
**What happened:** GigaToken promises to make language model tokenization roughly 1000x faster, potentially revolutionizing how we process and understand text.  
**Why it matters:** Faster tokenization means quicker AI responses and more efficient processing. If it works as advertised, it could be a game-changer for everything from chatbots to content creation.
