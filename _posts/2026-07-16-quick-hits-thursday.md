**1. Inkling: Our Open-Weights Model**

**What happened:** Thinking Machines unveiled Inkling, their new open-weights AI model, letting anyone peek under the hood and tweak it to their heart's content.

**Why it matters:** This move could democratize AI development, letting smaller players innovate without reinventing the wheel. But it also raises eyebrows about potential misuse — open doors swing both ways.

---

**2. Governments, Companies, Nonprofits Should Invest in Free, Open Source AI**

**What happened:** A new report argues that free, open-source AI is the key to equitable tech advancement and urges big players to pony up the resources.

**Why it matters:** If the big dogs listen, this could level the playing field, making AI accessible to more than just Silicon Valley elites. But will they? Or is this just wishful thinking?

---

**3. LLM Networking with MikroTik**

**What happened:** Greg's blog details how to use MikroTik routers for networking with Large Language Models, turning your home network into an AI powerhouse.

**Why it matters:** For the tinkerers and DIY enthusiasts, this is a game-changer. It puts AI capabilities into the hands of hobbyists, potentially sparking a wave of grassroots innovation. Just don't expect your router to write your next novel — yet.
